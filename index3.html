About Me

Hometown

I grew up in a small town called pearl river, about 30 minutes outside of New York city

It was a cute little town with a small village in the middle of it where I spent most of my childhood

Although I lived in Pearl River I went to school in the town over, Nanuet, because of the school borders

There was a surplus of shops and restaurants in the area around my town so there was always something to do

One of the highlights would definitely be the palisades mall
